Minchee Lab, your very first fab lab in Macau, is organizing monthly workshops to allow all to learn, make and share. Come and join us to discover and learn how to create interactive systems, 3d print your objects, etc… and more importantly to collaborate and have a unique experience with other people!

This year we are proposing to focus on 3 major topics: Arduino, Raspberry Pi and Digital Fabrication. Of course, this is in no way a limitation and we will adapt if needed.

  • Participants must register in advance to join a workshop
  • Participants should bring their own laptop (Mac or PC) but specific equipment such as Arduino, etc... will be provided during the workshop. However participants are encouraged to buy these kits (can buy during workshop) for personal usage and work on some projects.

Note: these workshops are mainly targeting adults and teenagers, if younger kids are interested let us know and we will try to work out some dedicated workshops for them.
