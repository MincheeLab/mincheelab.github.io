2014-2015 Workshops

Minchee Lab, your unique fab lab in Macau, is organizing monthly workshops to allow all to learn, make and share. Come and join us to discover and learn how to create interactive systems, 3d print your objects, etcâ€¦ and more importantly to have a unique experience with other people!

This year we are proposing to focus on 3 major topics: Arduino, Raspberry Pi and Digital Fabrication. Of course, this is in no way a limitation and we will adapt if needed.

Most of these workshops have the following information unless specified:

  • Time: 7pm to 10:30pm
  • Registration: 100MOP/workshop (maximum 20 seats).
  • Languages: English and Chinese.
Note: these workshops are mainly targeting adults and teenagers, if younger kids are interested let us know and we will try to work out some dedicated workshops for them.
