ZzzzzzMinchee Lab, your very first fab lab in Macau, is organizing monthly workshops to allow all to learn, make and share. Come and join us to discover and learn how to create interactive systems, 3d print your objects, etc… and more importantly to collaborate and have a unique experience with other people!

This year we are proposing to focus on 3 major topics: Arduino, Raspberry Pi and Digital Fabrication. Of course, this is in no way a limitation and we will adapt if needed.

  • Each workshop cost 100 (except specified)
  • Participant must register in advance to join a workshop
  • Participant must bring their own laptop but specific equipment such as Arduino, etc... will provided during the workshop. Participant can also choose to buy these kits for personal use.

Note: these workshops are mainly targeting adults and teenagers, if younger kids are interested let us know and we will try to work out some dedicated workshops for them.
